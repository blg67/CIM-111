Getting started

As a child I enjoyed helping my mother bake for the holidays. I enjoyed getting her the ingredients and helping her hand mix the batter. This was before we bought the Kitchen Aid. I enjoyed watching her place the batter into the pans and into the oven while she listened to Christmas music. Baking for my mom was a stress reliever from all the holiday stressers. All in all, because of those childhood memories I decided to make my own baking company. Also, because i realized how stressful it is around the holiday i started baking with booze. a deliouse cake with a touch of a boozie sensation for the holidays.

Boozie cupcakes

Margarita cupcakes

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour: 1c + 1/2c
  • Baking powder: 1tsp + 1/2tsp
  • Salt: 1/4tsp
  • Stick of butter: 1/2c
  • Granulated sugar: 1c
  • Egg: 2
  • Lime: 1 whole + 1/2
  • Tequila: 1c
  • Vanilla extract: 2tsp
  • Buttermilk: 1/2c

Directions

  1. In a large bowl place; flour, baking powder, and salt
  2. In mixer beat butter and sugar until combined and fluffy
  3. Add eggs one at a time in the mixer
  4. Add the Zest of one whole lime and lime juice of a lime and a half in the mixer
  5. Add vanilla and tequila to the mixer
  6. Add dry ingredients alternating with buttermilk in the mixer
  7. Bake

Baileys cupcakes

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour: 1c + 1/8c
  • Granulated sugar: 1c
  • Cocoa powder: 6tbsp
  • Baking soda: 3/4tsp
  • Baking powder: 1/2tsp
  • Salt: 1/4tsp
  • Vegitable oil: 1/2c
  • Strong coffee: 1/4c
  • Baileys: 1c
  • Egg: 2
  • Sour cream: 1/2c
  • Vanilla extract: 1tsp

Directions

  1. In mixer beat flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, salt, coffee, baileys, oil, and eggs until smooth and light
  2. Mix in sour cream and vanilla
  3. Bake

Frosting

American buttercream

Ingredients

  • Confectioner sugar: 32oz
  • Stick of butter: 4 sticks
  • Vanilla extract: 2tbsp
  • Heavy whipping cream: 1/3c

Directions

  1. Whip room temperature butter in mixer until fluffy
  2. Incorperate powder sugar 1c at a time
  3. Add vanilla
  4. Incorperate heavy whipping cream
  5. Place in a piping bag

Cream of coconut buttercream

Ingredients

  • Confectioner sugar: 32oz
  • Stick of butter: 4 sticks
  • Coconut extract: 2tbsp
  • Cream of coconut: 24oz

Directions

  1. Whip room temperature butter in mixer until fluffy
  2. Incorperate powder sugar 1c at a time
  3. Add cream of coconut
  4. Add coconut extract
  5. PLace in a piping bag

Designing your cupcakes

Depending what your customers order you'll have to design your cupcakes in the way they want it or if they gave you the option to use your imagination which will happen. In my experince I've been given the option the use my imagination and or i had direct orders in what they want the cupcakes to look like. What I've made in the past had been: snow white cupcakes, a big cupcake cake, halloween cupcakes, thanksgiving cupcakes, and more. I enjoy decorating cupcakes because they are so small compared to cakes you can put one or two things on them and it just makes it look complete. Cupcakes are easy to make and to decorate, but most of all fun!
